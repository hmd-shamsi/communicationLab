our milkshake, your milkshake
HAMAD MILKSHAKE is a fresh business launched in Abu Dhabi aiming to fulfill your sweet appetite! Creative enthusiast and chef Hamad AlShamsi found inspiration for the cafe when he accidentally dropped and oreo cookie into a 'secret ingredient' instead of the anticipated milk. The combination was so stunning that he decided to initiate an entire project based on it. The contemporary-lively menu consists of appetizing bites to yummy drinks that are guaranteed to make you fall in love with our offered selections!
